UltraDefrag
UltraDefrag is a disk defragmenter for Windows, which supports defragmentation of locked system files by running during the boot process. It is easy to use without any complicated scripting or a huge load of configuration settings. You can filter the files processed by size, number of fragments, file name and path. You can terminate the process early by specifying an execution time limit.

Conferences and Events

DjangoCon North America July 17 - 22
Come join us for DjangoCon US in Philadelphia, PA, an international community conference for the community by the community. It's held each year in North America, about the Django web framework. DjangoCon US has something for everyone, from the person who develops Django applications for a living, to the person who just tinkers in their spare time. Whether you're a Django expert, just started working in Django recently, or are interested in learning, we have talks and tutorials for you. DjangoCon US is a great place to learn, exchange ideas, and meet great people.

OpenStack Silicon Valley August 9 - 10
OpenStack Silicon Valley is being held in Mountain View, CA. In its third year, OpenStack Silicon Valley features thought leadership keynotes from open source infrastructure disruptors and IT leaders for Fortune 1000 enterprises on the edge of digital transformation.

Software is fundamentally redefining how enterprises compete, and a diverse and vibrant ecosystem is emerging to support this generational transformation. OpenStack Silicon Valley presents front-line use cases from organizations who are changing processes to embrace DevOps and other agile methods, using open cloud infrastructure to empower software developers. We have a fantastic lineup of speakers this year, including Diane Bryant from Intel, CIO of the Williams Formula 1 team, CIO of Staples, Head of Infra at Volkswagen Group, and Head of converged cloud at SAP.

The event format features keynotes and deep dives on the main stage, with sponsored breakouts that go into specific solutions and technologies driving the agile revolution. OpenStack Silicon Valley is a community event, with ways for community members of every size to participate. Join us in Mountain View at the Computer History Museum on August 9-10!

Linux Security Summit August 25 - 26
Join us at the Westin Harbour Castle in Toronto, Canada, for the Linux Security Summit (LSS), a technical forum for collaboration between Linux developers, researchers, and end users. Its primary aim is to foster community efforts in analyzing and solving Linux security challenges.

The LSS is a unique global event which provides the opportunity to present and discuss your work or research with key Linux security community members and maintainers. It's also useful for those who wish to keep up with the latest in Linux security development, and to provide input to the development process.
